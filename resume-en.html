About Me

The first of all I can talk about myself is that I'm passionate about technology, especially in the area of systems development, it's not to impress you that you're reading, it's just to express how satisfied I'm working with technology and how grateful I'm to have I had the opportunity to work with what I like and it really gives me pleasure.

I began my journey around my 15 years of age, teaching basic computer classes in my hometown, always had the facility to learn everything related to technology, and with this I dedicated myself to teaching other people, even before I started my degree. I also spent some time setting up and configuring computers, where I gained extensive experience in hardware and peripherals. In the meantime, I built sites, not very professional, but I was starting on what I would later perfect myself.

In 2003, I started my degree in Computer Science from the Catholic University of Goiás, deepening my knowledge that I use to this day in my day-to-day life. At the university I was the tutor of the LM (Assembly Language) discipline with focus on Assembly for low level programming. I also had the opportunity to be the president of the Academic Center of Computer Science. During the college, I interned in two companies in the city of Goiânia, where I was able to put into practice the knowledge I acquired at the university.

In 2007 I graduated and moved to the city of Brasília, where I live until today, serving several federal government bodies and public companies. In 2008 I got married and in 2013 my beautiful daughter Sophia was born, who today, along with my wife, are my inhaling to be able to continue improving my knowledge and growing up day after day.
